When founders find us

You have a strong point of view. It's not reaching the market.

You know what you believe and where the category is going. It's living in your head, not in the market. Your competitors are filling that space.

02

You know your ICP. You can't get in front of them.

You know exactly who needs to hear this. The path to those buyers, partners, or investors isn't clear, warm, or moving fast enough.
